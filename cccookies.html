Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookies!

By: Tori Stell

Notes Jump to Recipe

These are some of the best cookies I have ever had! I stumbled upon them while mindlessly sifting through and saving recipes on Pinterest. They caught my attention because they have cornstarch in them which make them so incredibly tender. I will say these are the quintasential chocolate chip cookies that everyone wants and hopes for. There is a bit of a crunch on the outer edge with a soft gooey center and the most perfect texture. Try them and you will be a happy camper!
